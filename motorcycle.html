Motorcycle Modification

Project information

  • Hardware: auxiliary lights, engine temperature gauge, ambient temperature gauge, anti-thief system, voltmeter gauge, electrical relays/fuses/switches, muffler, engine.
  • Software: Power Commander 5
  • Project date: Jan, 2021 - Jul, 2021

This is the longest project I've ever had on my bike. Before ordering parts, I had to estimate their size in order to model them in Solidworks to get sizing ideas. In addition, I retrofitted a few parts into my handcrafted brackets. In terms of electricity, I worked hard to route entire electric wires into a separate department without interfering with OEM wiring. My bike's electrical equipment always includes a full standard of electricity, such as a relay and fuse box.
