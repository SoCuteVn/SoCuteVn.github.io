2-DOF robot arm

Project information

  • Hardware: Arduino Uno R3, potentiometer, servo motor, LCD dipslay, 2-DOF arm
  • Software: C, Arduino IDE
  • Project date: Apr, 2022 - May, 2022

This project created to fully control the endpoint of the 2-DOF robotic arm by 2 potentiometers including manual and computer-coded calculations.
